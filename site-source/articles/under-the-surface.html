An interactive spatial data experience by Field.io studio for IBM Randori, allowing you to see through the eyes of a hacker.

An adventure into spatial data visualisation, Field.io contacted me to help them out with the task of developing an interactive system that visualises real attack surface data from a hacker's point of view.

This tool would travel to digital security conferences all over the world, and win multiple awards allowing IBM to explain their Randori product to their customers in a dynamic and immersive way.
